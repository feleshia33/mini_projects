Education and Experience

Passionate architect with a focus on sustainability, renewable energy and reduced ecological footprint. BS. in Architecture from pratt Institute New York. Experience with low-cost, energy efficient homes, wooden-frame construction and hemp-based insulation materials.

Worked on commercial buildings valued in the range of $15-50 million. Created 2D drawings of floor plans, sections using AutoCAD. Performed research for lead architect on specifications, materials, building codes. Assisted Project Managers with reports, cost estimates and time tables.
